Philosophy

Beyond the Empirical

Our project is born from a simple realization: the tools we use to measure the universe are limited by the universe itself. To understand what lies beyond, we must transcend traditional methodologies.

We combine theoretical physics with philosophical speculation to create a framework for exploring "Externalities"—phenomena that exist outside the relativistic constraints of our space-time.

Framework

The Mathematics of the Void

Our work is guided by three primary theoretical models that bridge the gap between known physics and the Beyond.

01

The Membrane Hypothesis

Suggests our universe exists on a 3-dimensional membrane (brane) floating in a higher-dimensional bulk. The Beyond is the space between these branes.

02

Eternal Inflation Model

Proposes that inflation never truly stopped, but continues in the "Beyond," creating an infinite sea of "pocket universes" of which we are but one.

03

Information Preservation

A theory stating that no information is ever lost, even beyond an event horizon. This implies that the Beyond is a vast, readable archive of all that has passed.
