# Beyond Reality 🌌

> *"Are you the dreamer, or the dream?"*

**Beyond Reality** is a hyper-realistic, open-source web platform dedicated to visualizing the ultimate extremes of astrophysics, quantum mechanics, cosmology, and philosophical paradoxes. Built entirely with Vanilla JavaScript, Three.js, and custom GLSL Shaders, it offers an immersive "scientific instrument" aesthetic to explore the unknown.

## 🚀 Features & Simulators
- **The Quantum Foam:** A 3D fractal raymarching shader exploring the Planck scale.
- **Dyson Sphere Simulator:** Watch a Type II Kardashev Civilization build a megastructure around a star.
- **The Boltzmann Brain:** Simulate the end of the universe and the spontaneous generation of consciousness from quantum static.
- **Large Hadron Collider:** Recreate the collision of high-energy particles at 99.999999% the speed of light.
- **The Simulation Hypothesis:** An interactive UI that degrades the integrity of our universe to reveal the underlying cybernetic grid.
- **Daily Paradoxes:** A curated series of philosophical thought experiments, starting with Zhuangzi's Butterfly Dream.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
- **Frontend Core:** HTML5, CSS3 (Glassmorphism UI), Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+).
- **3D Graphics:** [Three.js](https://threejs.org/) for rendering celestial and quantum phenomena.
- **Shaders:** Custom GLSL Vertex and Fragment shaders for chaotic/organic systems.
