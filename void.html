What is the Void?

The "Void" is our term for the space between branes. It is not empty space; it is the absence of space-time itself. In this region, physical laws as we know them do not exist.

"The Void is the canvas upon which the universe is painted. To understand the paint, we must first understand the canvas."

Dark Flow Vectors

We have observed galaxies being pulled toward a specific point in the void, outside the observable universe. This "Dark Flow" suggests the existence of a massive attractor in the Beyond.

  • Acceleration: 600 km/s
  • Direction: Centaurus/Hydra
  • Cause: Extra-universal gravity
