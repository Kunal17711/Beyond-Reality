Glossary

Defining the vocabulary of the infinite.

Brane (Membrane)
A multi-dimensional object in string theory and related theories. Our observable universe is hypothesized to be a 3-dimensional brane.
CMB (Cosmic Microwave Background)
The electromagnetic radiation remaining from an early stage of the universe in Big Bang cosmology. It is a key tool for observing the edge of the known universe.
Dark Flow
A term used to describe a possible non-random component of the peculiar velocity of galaxy clusters, potentially caused by matter outside the observable universe.
Event Horizon
A boundary in space-time beyond which events cannot affect an observer. In our context, it often refers to the limit of the observable universe.
Multiverse
The hypothetical set of all possible universes, including the one in which we live. Together, these universes comprise everything that exists.
Singularity
A point at which a function takes an infinite value, especially in space-time where gravitational forces become infinite.
Void (The Beyond)
In our platform's context, the theoretical "space" that contains our expanding universe and potentially others.
